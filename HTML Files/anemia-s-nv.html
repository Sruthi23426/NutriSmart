About Anemia

South Indian non-vegetarian diets provide rich sources of iron such as fish, eggs, and chicken, combined with traditional meals like rice and curries to support healthy hemoglobin levels.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Fish (rich in iron and omega-3)
  • Eggs (good source of protein and B12)
  • Chicken (lean protein)
  • Green leafy vegetables (cooked)
  • Beetroot and dates
  • Fruits like pomegranate and orange

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Tea/coffee immediately after meals
  • Highly processed foods
  • Excess fried items
  • Refined sugar

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Egg dosa / egg upma / idli + egg curry
  • Mid-Morning: Fruits / coconut water / fresh juice
  • Lunch: Fish curry + rice / chicken curry + vegetables / egg curry + rice
  • Evening: Boiled eggs / nuts / sprouts
  • Dinner: Grilled fish / chicken + vegetables / light egg curry

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Walking
  • Light yoga
  • Breathing exercises

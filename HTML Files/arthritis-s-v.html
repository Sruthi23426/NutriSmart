About Arthritis

South Indian vegetarian diets can be made anti-inflammatory by using millets, coconut, and spices like turmeric and curry leaves.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Millets (ragi, jowar)
  • Idli and dosa (fermented foods)
  • Coconut-based dishes
  • Vegetables
  • Curd
  • Spices like turmeric

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Deep fried snacks (vada, bajji)
  • Excess rice
  • Processed foods
  • Sugary sweets

Diet Plan 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Idli + sambar / ragi dosa / vegetable upma
  • Mid-Morning: Tender coconut water / fruit
  • Lunch: Brown rice + sambar + poriyal + curd / millet meals
  • Snack: Sundal / nuts / herbal tea
  • Dinner: Vegetable soup / chapati + curry / dosa + chutney

Exercises 🏃

  • Yoga
  • Walking
  • Breathing exercises
  • Light stretching

About Asthma

Non-vegetarian foods provide protein and essential nutrients that support lung function and immunity. Fresh, lean meats and anti-inflammatory foods help manage asthma symptoms effectively.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Lean chicken and fish
  • Eggs (rich in protein)
  • Fruits like apple, orange
  • Leafy vegetables
  • Ginger, garlic (anti-inflammatory)
  • Warm fluids (soups, herbal tea)

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Processed meats
  • Cold foods and beverages
  • Excess fried foods
  • Preserved and packaged food

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Boiled eggs / omelette / oats + milk / toast + eggs
  • Mid-Morning: Fruits / warm water / nuts
  • Lunch: Chicken curry + roti / fish curry + rice / egg curry + vegetables
  • Evening: Boiled eggs / fruit / herbal tea
  • Dinner: Grilled chicken / fish + vegetables / egg bhurji + roti

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Breathing exercises (pranayama)
  • Walking
  • Light yoga

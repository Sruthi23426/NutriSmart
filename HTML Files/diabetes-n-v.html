About Diabetes

A vegetarian diet rich in fiber and low in refined carbs helps control blood sugar. Whole grains and plant protein are key for stability.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Whole wheat roti
  • Brown rice
  • Paneer (low fat)
  • Dal and legumes
  • Leafy vegetables
  • Sprouts

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Sweets and sugary foods
  • Fried snacks
  • Refined flour items
  • Packaged juices

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast Options: Oats / vegetable poha (less oil) / sprouts salad / dal chilla
  • Mid-Morning: Guava / apple / handful of nuts
  • Lunch Options: 1–2 roti + dal / chole / rajma + sabzi + salad
  • Evening Snack: Roasted chana / sprouts / herbal tea
  • Dinner Options: Vegetable soup / light sabzi + salad / small portion khichdi
  • Optional: Buttermilk / jeera water

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Walking (30 min)
  • Yoga
  • Light cardio

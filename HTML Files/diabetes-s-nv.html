NutriSmart

About Diabetes

Adding lean protein like fish, eggs, and chicken helps reduce blood sugar spikes and improves insulin response when combined with a balanced diet.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Grilled fish or fish curry (low oil)
  • Boiled eggs
  • Skinless chicken
  • Millets and brown rice
  • Vegetable sambar and salads
  • Nuts and seeds

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Fried chicken or fish
  • Excess red meat
  • Large portions of white rice
  • Heavy coconut-based gravies
  • Processed meats

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast Options: Idli (2) / dosa (less oil) + egg / vegetable omelette / upma
  • Mid-Morning: Coconut water / guava / small fruit portion
  • Lunch Options: Brown rice (small portion) + fish curry / chicken (low oil) + vegetable poriyal
  • Evening Snack: Boiled egg / sundal / buttermilk
  • Dinner Options: Grilled fish / egg whites + vegetables / light soup
  • Optional: Rasam / unsweetened herbal drinks

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Walking or jogging
  • Yoga and breathing exercises
  • Cycling

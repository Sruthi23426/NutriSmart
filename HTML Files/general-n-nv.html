About Healthy Lifestyle

A balanced non-vegetarian diet provides high-quality protein and essential nutrients when consumed in moderation and with healthy cooking methods.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Chicken (grilled/boiled)
  • Fish (rich in omega-3)
  • Eggs
  • Whole grains
  • Vegetables
  • Fruits

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Fried meat
  • Processed meat
  • Excess oil
  • Junk food

Diet Plan 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Boiled eggs + toast / omelette / oats + fruit / egg sandwich
  • Mid-Morning: Fruit juice / banana / coconut water
  • Lunch: Chicken curry + roti / fish curry + rice / egg curry + sabzi
  • Snack: Nuts / boiled eggs / sprouts
  • Dinner: Grilled chicken + vegetables / soup / light curry + roti

Exercises 🏃

  • Walking
  • Jogging
  • Strength training
  • Sports (badminton, cricket)

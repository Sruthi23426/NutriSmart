About Heart Disease

Non-vegetarian foods can be included in moderation with a focus on lean protein and low fat cooking methods to reduce cholesterol levels.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Grilled chicken
  • Fish (rich in omega-3)
  • Egg whites
  • Whole grains
  • Vegetables
  • Fruits

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Red meat (excess)
  • Fried chicken
  • Butter & cream
  • Processed meat
  • Deep fried foods

Diet Plan 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Egg whites + toast / oats + fruits / boiled eggs
  • Mid-Morning: Fruit smoothie / apple / buttermilk
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken + roti + salad / fish curry + rice
  • Snack: Nuts / boiled eggs / sprouts
  • Dinner: Chicken soup / vegetable sabzi + roti

Exercises 🏃

  • Walking
  • Swimming
  • Light jogging
  • Yoga

About Heart Disease

South Indian non-veg diets can be heart-healthy when focusing on fish and lean meats while avoiding fried and oily preparations.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Fish (grilled or curry)
  • Egg whites
  • Chicken (grilled)
  • Millets
  • Vegetables
  • Fruits

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Fried fish
  • Red meat (excess)
  • High oil curries
  • Processed meats

Diet Plan 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Egg dosa / idli + boiled eggs / upma
  • Mid-Morning: Fruit / coconut water
  • Lunch: Fish curry + rice / chicken curry + millet
  • Snack: Boiled eggs / nuts
  • Dinner: Chicken soup / vegetable stew + roti

Exercises 🏃

  • Swimming
  • Walking
  • Low-impact aerobics
  • Yoga

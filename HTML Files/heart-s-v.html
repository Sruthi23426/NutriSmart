About Heart Disease

South Indian diets can be heart-friendly when made with millets, less oil, and traditional fermented foods like idli and dosa.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Millets (ragi, jowar)
  • Idli and dosa
  • Vegetables
  • Coconut (limited)
  • Legumes
  • Curd (low-fat)

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Deep fried vada/bajji
  • Excess coconut oil
  • White rice (excess)
  • Processed snacks

Diet Plan 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Idli + sambar / ragi dosa / vegetable upma
  • Mid-Morning: Coconut water / fruit
  • Lunch: Millet rice + sambar + vegetable poriyal
  • Snack: Sundal / nuts / herbal tea
  • Dinner: Vegetable soup / chapati + curry

Exercises 🏃

  • Walking
  • Yoga
  • Light stretching
  • Breathing exercises

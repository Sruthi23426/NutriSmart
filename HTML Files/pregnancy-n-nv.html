About Pregnancy Nutrition

During pregnancy, the body requires additional nutrients such as iron, calcium, folic acid, and protein to support fetal growth and maternal health. A balanced vegetarian diet can meet these needs when planned carefully.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Lean chicken
  • Eggs
  • Fish (low mercury)
  • Whole grains
  • Vegetables and fruits

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Unpasteurized dairy products
  • Excess caffeine
  • Highly processed foods
  • Street food (risk of infection)

Flagged Foods ⚠️

Avoid skipping meals. Long gaps can lead to low energy levels and affect fetal nourishment.

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast Options: Boiled eggs + toast / omelette + roti / oats with milk / vegetable upma
  • Mid-Morning Options: Fruits / coconut water / nuts
  • Lunch Options: 2 roti + chicken curry + sabzi / rice + fish curry + vegetables / roti + egg curry + salad
  • Evening Snack Options: Boiled egg / sprouts / roasted snacks / buttermilk
  • Dinner Options: Grilled chicken + vegetables / light fish curry + salad / roti + egg bhurji
  • Healthy Drinks: Buttermilk / lemon water / fresh juice

Why This Plan Works 📊

  • Provides high-quality protein
  • Supports fetal brain development
  • Improves iron levels

Recommended Activities 🧘

  • Light walking
  • Prenatal yoga (doctor approved)
  • Breathing exercises

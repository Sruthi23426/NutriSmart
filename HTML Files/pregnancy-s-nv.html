About Pregnancy Nutrition

During pregnancy, the body requires additional nutrients such as iron, calcium, folic acid, and protein to support fetal growth and maternal health. A balanced vegetarian diet can meet these needs when planned carefully.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Fish (rich in omega-3)
  • Eggs
  • Rice + sambar
  • Vegetables
  • Buttermilk

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Unpasteurized dairy products
  • Excess caffeine
  • Highly processed foods
  • Street food (risk of infection)

Flagged Foods ⚠️

Avoid skipping meals. Long gaps can lead to low energy levels and affect fetal nourishment.

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast Options: Idli + egg / dosa + omelette / oats + boiled eggs
  • Mid-Morning: Fruit + nuts
  • Lunch Options: Rice + fish curry + vegetables / millet rice + chicken curry / rice + egg curry + poriyal
  • Evening: Sprouts / buttermilk
  • Dinner Options: Grilled fish + vegetables / light chicken curry + salad / egg bhurji + roti
  • Optional: Coconut water / fresh juice

Why This Plan Works 📊

  • Omega-3 supports brain development
  • Balanced meals provide nutrients
  • Supports maternal health

Recommended Activities 🧘

  • Light walking
  • Prenatal yoga (doctor approved)
  • Breathing exercises

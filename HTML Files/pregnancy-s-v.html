About Pregnancy Nutrition

During pregnancy, the body requires additional nutrients such as iron, calcium, folic acid, and protein to support fetal growth and maternal health. South Indian diets include fermented foods and rice-based meals. Balanced intake ensures proper nutrient absorption and digestion.

  • Idli, dosa (fermented)
  • Sambar and vegetables
  • Milk and curd
  • Fruits
  • Millets

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Unpasteurized dairy products
  • Excess caffeine
  • Highly processed foods
  • Street food (risk of infection)

Flagged Foods ⚠️

Avoid skipping meals. Long gaps can lead to low energy levels and affect fetal nourishment.

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast Options: Idli / dosa / ragi porridge / oats upma
  • Mid-Morning: Fruit + nuts
  • Lunch Options: Rice + sambar + poriyal / millet rice + veg curry / curd rice + vegetables
  • Dinner Options: Vegetable upma / light dosa / soup + salad
  • Optional: Coconut water / fresh juice

Why This Plan Works 📊

  • Fermented foods improve digestion
  • Provides calcium and iron
  • Maintains energy levels

Recommended Activities 🧘

  • Light walking
  • Prenatal yoga (doctor approved)
  • Breathing exercises

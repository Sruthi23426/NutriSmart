About Thyroid

Non-vegetarian foods provide essential nutrients like iodine, selenium, and protein that support thyroid hormone production and metabolism regulation.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Eggs (rich in iodine and selenium)
  • Fish (especially tuna, salmon)
  • Chicken (lean protein)
  • Dairy products (milk, curd, paneer)
  • Whole grains
  • Cooked green vegetables

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Excess fried chicken or oily meat
  • Processed meat
  • Excess soy products
  • Refined sugar and junk food

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Boiled eggs / omelette + toast / oats + milk
  • Mid-Morning: Fruits / nuts
  • Lunch: Chicken curry + roti / fish curry + rice / egg curry + vegetables
  • Evening: Boiled eggs / nuts / fruit chaat
  • Dinner: Grilled chicken / fish + vegetables / egg bhurji + roti

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Walking
  • Yoga
  • Light strength training

About Thyroid

South Indian non-vegetarian diets provide essential nutrients like iodine and protein while maintaining traditional eating patterns beneficial for thyroid health.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Eggs (dosa + egg combinations)
  • Fish (rich in omega-3 and iodine)
  • Chicken (lean protein)
  • Curd and buttermilk
  • Rice and whole grains
  • Cooked vegetables

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Deep fried fish/chicken
  • Highly processed foods
  • Excess coconut oil in large amounts
  • Refined sugar

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Egg dosa / omelette + idli / upma + egg
  • Mid-Morning: Fruits / coconut water
  • Lunch: Fish curry + rice / chicken curry + vegetables / egg curry
  • Evening: Boiled eggs / nuts / sprouts
  • Dinner: Grilled fish / chicken + vegetables / light egg curry

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Walking
  • Yoga
  • Light workouts

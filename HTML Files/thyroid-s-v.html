About Thyroid

A South Indian vegetarian diet can support thyroid health by providing essential nutrients like iodine, selenium, and fiber while maintaining traditional balanced meals.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Iodized salt
  • Milk, curd, buttermilk
  • Whole grains (rice, ragi, millets)
  • Cooked vegetables
  • Nuts (especially almonds, walnuts)
  • Fruits like banana, apple

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Raw cabbage, cauliflower (cruciferous vegetables)
  • Highly processed foods
  • Excess soy products
  • Refined sugar

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Idli + sambar / dosa + chutney / ragi porridge / vegetable upma
  • Mid-Morning: Fruits / coconut water / soaked nuts
  • Lunch: Rice + sambar + vegetables + curd / rasam + rice + poriyal
  • Evening: Roasted chana / nuts / herbal tea
  • Dinner: Vegetable khichdi / dosa + chutney / light rice + dal

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Walking
  • Yoga
  • Light physical activity

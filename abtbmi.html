Check Your Health Progress

Tracking your progress helps you understand how your body is responding to healthier choices. NutriSmart visually represents your improvements so you stay motivated, consistent, and confident in your wellness journey.

BMI Improvement Over Time

This chart shows how your Body Mass Index changes when you consistently follow NutriSmart’s guidance. A gradual and steady improvement indicates healthy weight management rather than extreme or unsafe weight loss. Monitoring BMI over time helps ensure long-term balance and sustainable health.

Weight Change Trend

Your weight trend reflects how well your diet and activity levels align with your health goals. Small fluctuations are normal, but a consistent positive trend indicates better metabolism and improved body composition. This visual helps users stay patient and trust the process.

Diet Consistency Analysis

This section highlights how consistently you follow your recommended diet plan. Balanced nutrition over time improves digestion, energy levels, and immunity. Consistency matters more than perfection, and this chart reinforces healthy habits.

Exercise Performance Growth

Regular physical activity strengthens muscles, improves heart health, and boosts mental well-being. This chart reflects improvements in activity frequency and endurance. Even small increases in movement contribute significantly to overall fitness.

Overall Health Score

The overall health score combines diet, exercise, and BMI data into a single meaningful indicator. It helps users understand their wellness status at a glance. A rising score indicates positive lifestyle changes and improved long-term health outcomes.

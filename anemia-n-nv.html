About Anemia

Non-vegetarian foods provide highly absorbable iron and vitamin B12, which are crucial for treating anemia and improving hemoglobin levels.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Chicken liver
  • Eggs
  • Fish (salmon, tuna)
  • Lean red meat
  • Spinach and beetroot
  • Dates and raisins

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Tea/coffee immediately after meals
  • Highly processed foods
  • Excess fried meat
  • Refined sugar

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Egg omelette / boiled eggs / oats + fruits / toast + eggs
  • Mid-Morning: Pomegranate / orange juice / beetroot juice
  • Lunch: Chicken curry + roti / fish curry + rice / egg curry + salad
  • Evening: Boiled eggs / nuts / fruit bowl
  • Dinner: Grilled chicken / fish + vegetables / egg bhurji + roti

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Walking
  • Light yoga
  • Breathing exercises

About Anemia

Anemia is a condition caused by low hemoglobin levels, leading to fatigue and weakness. A vegetarian diet rich in iron, vitamin C, and folate helps improve red blood cell production.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Spinach, methi, kale
  • Beetroot, carrots
  • Dates, raisins, jaggery
  • Legumes (rajma, chole)
  • Fortified cereals
  • Milk and paneer

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Tea/coffee with meals
  • Processed junk food
  • Excess dairy with iron-rich meals

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast Options: Oats with dates / vegetable upma / besan chilla / paratha + spinach
  • Mid-Morning Options: Pomegranate / orange juice / soaked raisins / beetroot juice
  • Lunch Options: 2 roti + palak paneer / rajma + rice + salad / chole + roti + beetroot
  • Evening Snack Options: Sprouts salad / roasted chana / fruit bowl / nuts
  • Dinner Options: Vegetable khichdi / paneer curry + roti / soup + salad / dal + roti

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Light walking
  • Yoga (improves blood flow)
  • Breathing exercises

About Anemia

South Indian vegetarian diets can be rich in iron by including millets, green leafy vegetables, and legumes along with vitamin C-rich foods to improve iron absorption and boost hemoglobin levels.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Ragi, millets, and whole grains
  • Spinach, drumstick leaves, other greens
  • Lentils, dal, chickpeas
  • Beetroot and jaggery
  • Dates, raisins
  • Fruits rich in Vitamin C (orange, lemon)

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Tea/coffee immediately after meals
  • Highly processed foods
  • Excess refined sugar
  • Low-iron junk foods

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Ragi dosa / idli + sambar / vegetable upma
  • Mid-Morning: Coconut water / fruits / beetroot juice
  • Lunch: Rice + sambar + greens / millet + curry + vegetables
  • Evening: Sundal / roasted peanuts / sprouts
  • Dinner: Vegetable upma / dosa + chutney / soup

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Walking
  • Yoga
  • Light breathing exercises

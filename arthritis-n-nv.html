NutriSmart

About Arthritis

Including lean protein and omega-3 rich foods helps reduce inflammation and supports joint health.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Grilled fish (rich in omega-3)
  • Boiled eggs
  • Lean chicken
  • Leafy vegetables
  • Fruits
  • Whole grains

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Processed meat
  • Deep fried food
  • Excess red meat
  • Alcohol
  • Sugary foods

Diet Plan 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Egg omelette + whole wheat toast / oats + boiled eggs / vegetable poha
  • Mid-Morning: Fruit bowl / buttermilk / coconut water
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken + roti + salad / fish curry + rice / dal + sabzi
  • Snack: Nuts / boiled eggs / sprouts
  • Dinner: Chicken soup / light sabzi + roti / khichdi

Exercises 🏃

  • Swimming
  • Walking
  • Light strength training
  • Stretching

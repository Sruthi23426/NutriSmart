About Arthritis

Arthritis causes joint pain and inflammation. A diet rich in anti-inflammatory foods like whole grains, fruits, and healthy fats helps reduce stiffness and pain.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Turmeric milk
  • Whole grains (brown rice, oats)
  • Green leafy vegetables
  • Nuts (almonds, walnuts)
  • Ginger and garlic
  • Fruits like berries and papaya

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Processed foods
  • Deep fried snacks
  • Refined sugar
  • Excess salt
  • Packaged snacks

Diet Plan 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Vegetable upma / oats / moong dal chilla / poha
  • Mid-Morning: Fruit smoothie / coconut water / fruit salad
  • Lunch: Brown rice + dal + sabzi / roti + paneer curry + salad
  • Snack: Roasted peanuts / sprouts / herbal tea
  • Dinner: Khichdi / vegetable soup / chapati + sabzi
  • Optional: Warm turmeric water

Exercises 🏃

  • Gentle yoga
  • Stretching exercises
  • Swimming
  • Walking (20–30 min)

About Arthritis

Fish and seafood in South Indian diets provide omega-3 fatty acids that help reduce inflammation and joint pain.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Fish curry (omega-3 rich)
  • Eggs
  • Chicken (grilled)
  • Millets
  • Vegetables
  • Curd

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Fried seafood
  • Processed meat
  • Excess oil
  • Sugary foods

Diet Plan 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Egg dosa / idli + boiled eggs / upma
  • Mid-Morning: Coconut water / fruit juice
  • Lunch: Fish curry + rice / chicken curry + millet / sambar + egg
  • Snack: Boiled eggs / nuts / buttermilk
  • Dinner: Chicken soup / vegetable stew + roti / light fish curry

Exercises 🏃

  • Swimming
  • Walking
  • Low-impact aerobics
  • Stretching

About Asthma

Asthma is a chronic respiratory condition that causes inflammation and narrowing of airways. A diet rich in anti-inflammatory and immune-supporting foods can help reduce symptoms and improve breathing.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Turmeric milk
  • Ginger and garlic
  • Fresh fruits (apple, papaya, banana)
  • Green vegetables (cooked)
  • Whole grains
  • Nuts and seeds

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Cold beverages
  • Fried and oily foods
  • Processed snacks
  • Excess dairy (can increase mucus)
  • Artificial preservatives

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast Options: Oats with fruits / vegetable poha / upma / banana smoothie
  • Mid-Morning: Warm water / fruit / soaked nuts
  • Lunch Options: 2 roti + dal + cooked vegetables / khichdi + salad / paneer + roti
  • Evening: Herbal tea / roasted chana / fruit
  • Dinner Options: Light soup / vegetable curry + roti / khichdi

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Breathing exercises (pranayama)
  • Yoga
  • Light walking

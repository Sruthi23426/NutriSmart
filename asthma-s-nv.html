About Asthma

South Indian non-vegetarian diets include fish and eggs which provide omega-3 fatty acids and protein, helping reduce inflammation and improve lung function.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Fish (rich in omega-3)
  • Eggs
  • Lean chicken
  • Fruits like apple, orange
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Warm fluids (soups, herbal tea)

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Cold foods and beverages
  • Processed meats
  • Deep fried foods
  • Packaged snacks

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Egg dosa / omelette / idli + egg curry
  • Mid-Morning: Fruit / warm water / coconut water
  • Lunch: Fish curry + rice / chicken curry + vegetables / egg curry
  • Evening: Boiled eggs / nuts / herbal tea
  • Dinner: Grilled fish / chicken + vegetables / light egg curry

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Breathing exercises (pranayama)
  • Walking
  • Light yoga

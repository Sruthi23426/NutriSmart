About Asthma

South Indian vegetarian diets focus on light, nutritious, and easy-to-digest foods that help reduce inflammation and support better breathing.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Fruits like apple, orange
  • Leafy vegetables
  • Turmeric, ginger, garlic
  • Whole grains and millets
  • Warm fluids (herbal tea, soups)
  • Nuts and seeds

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Cold drinks and ice creams
  • Processed and packaged foods
  • Deep fried foods
  • Excess dairy (if triggering symptoms)

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Idli / dosa / vegetable upma / ragi porridge
  • Mid-Morning: Coconut water / fruit / warm water
  • Lunch: Rice + sambar + vegetables / curd rice / millet + curry
  • Evening: Sundal / roasted peanuts / herbal tea
  • Dinner: Vegetable soup / light dosa / upma

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Pranayama (breathing exercises)
  • Walking
  • Light yoga

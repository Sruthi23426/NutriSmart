About Hypertension

Hypertension (high blood pressure) is often linked to excess salt intake, stress, and low physical activity. In North Indian diets, pickles, papads, and salty snacks can contribute to increased sodium levels. Managing portion size and reducing salt is key.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Grilled chicken (low salt)
  • Boiled eggs
  • Fish (lightly cooked)
  • Whole wheat roti
  • Vegetables and salads
  • Low-fat dairy

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Pickles and papad
  • Packaged and processed foods
  • Excess salt in cooking
  • Fried snacks
  • Instant noodles and soups

Daily Diet Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Boiled eggs + toast / oats / poha
  • Mid-morning: Fruit or coconut water
  • Lunch: Roti + grilled chicken/fish + sabzi
  • Evening: Roasted snacks / sprouts
  • Dinner: Light protein + vegetables

Why This Plan Works 📊

  • Lean protein supports heart health
  • Low salt prevents BP spikes
  • Balanced nutrients improve circulation

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Brisk walking (30 min daily)
  • Yoga (pranayama, breathing exercises)
  • Light stretching

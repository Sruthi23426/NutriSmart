About Hypertension

South Indian diets often include rice, coconut, and fermented foods. While healthy, excess salt in chutneys and pickles can increase blood pressure. Moderation and balance are essential.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Idli, dosa (low salt batter)
  • Vegetable sambar
  • Brown rice (controlled portions)
  • Leafy vegetables
  • Buttermilk
  • Fruits like banana and papaya

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Pickles and papad
  • Packaged and processed foods
  • Excess salt in cooking
  • Fried snacks
  • Instant noodles and soups

Daily Diet Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Idli / dosa / upma / pongal
  • Mid-morning: Tender coconut water / fruit
  • Lunch: Rice + sambar + poriyal + curd
  • Evening: Sundal / buttermilk
  • Dinner: Light dosa / vegetable soup

Why This Plan Works 📊

  • Fermented foods improve digestion
  • Low salt reduces BP risk
  • Balanced meals prevent spikes

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Brisk walking (30 min daily)
  • Yoga (pranayama, breathing exercises)
  • Light stretching

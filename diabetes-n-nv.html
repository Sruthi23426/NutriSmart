About Diabetes

Diabetes affects how your body processes blood sugar. A balanced diet with controlled carbohydrates and sufficient protein helps maintain stable glucose levels. Including lean non-vegetarian sources can improve protein intake without causing sugar spikes.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Whole wheat roti
  • Grilled chicken
  • Boiled eggs
  • Fish curry (low oil)
  • Green vegetables
  • Dal and legumes

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Sugary desserts
  • Deep fried chicken
  • Processed meats (sausages, salami)
  • White rice (excess)
  • Soft drinks

Flagged Foods ⚠️

Egg yolks and red meat should be consumed in moderation. Prefer lean protein like chicken breast and fish.

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast Options: Oats / vegetable poha / upma / sprouts salad
  • Mid-Morning: Seasonal fruits (papaya, apple) or buttermilk
  • Lunch Options: 2 roti + dal / chole / rajma + sabzi + salad
  • Evening Snack: Roasted chana / sprouts / herbal tea
  • Dinner Options: Vegetable soup / khichdi / light sabzi + salad
  • Optional: Lemon water / jeera water

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • 30–40 min brisk walking
  • Light jogging
  • Yoga and breathing exercises
  • Strength training (2–3 times/week)

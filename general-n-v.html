About Healthy Lifestyle

A balanced vegetarian diet helps maintain energy, supports immunity, and keeps the body healthy. Focus on whole foods, hydration, and regular meals.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Whole grains (roti, brown rice)
  • Fresh fruits
  • Green vegetables
  • Milk and curd
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Dal and legumes

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Excess sugar
  • Junk food
  • Deep fried snacks
  • Processed food

Diet Plan 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Vegetable poha / oats / paratha + curd / fruit smoothie
  • Mid-Morning: Apple / banana / nuts / buttermilk
  • Lunch: 2 roti + dal + sabzi + salad / rice + rajma / paneer curry
  • Snack: Roasted chana / sprouts / herbal tea
  • Dinner: Light sabzi + roti / khichdi / vegetable soup

Exercises 🏃

  • Walking (30 min)
  • Yoga
  • Stretching
  • Light strength training

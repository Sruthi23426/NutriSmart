About Healthy Lifestyle

Non-vegetarian South Indian diets can be healthy when combined with fish, lean meats, and balanced meals rich in vegetables and grains.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Fish curry
  • Chicken (grilled)
  • Eggs
  • Millets
  • Vegetables
  • Fruits

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Fried meat
  • Processed foods
  • Excess oil
  • Junk food

Diet Plan 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Egg dosa / omelette + toast / idli + eggs
  • Mid-Morning: Fruit / coconut water / juice
  • Lunch: Fish curry + rice / chicken curry + millet / egg curry
  • Snack: Boiled eggs / nuts / buttermilk
  • Dinner: Chicken soup / fish stew / vegetable + roti

Exercises 🏃

  • Swimming
  • Walking
  • Jogging
  • Sports activities

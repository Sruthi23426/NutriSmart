About Healthy Lifestyle

South Indian vegetarian meals are rich in fiber and probiotics. Including millets and fermented foods supports digestion and overall health.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Idli, dosa, upma
  • Millets (ragi, jowar)
  • Vegetables
  • Fruits
  • Curd and buttermilk
  • Legumes

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Deep fried snacks
  • Excess oil
  • Junk food
  • Refined sugar

Diet Plan 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Idli + sambar / dosa + chutney / upma / pongal
  • Mid-Morning: Coconut water / fruit
  • Lunch: Rice + sambar + poriyal + curd / millet meals
  • Snack: Sundal / nuts / herbal tea
  • Dinner: Dosa / chapati + curry / vegetable soup

Exercises 🏃

  • Yoga
  • Walking
  • Breathing exercises
  • Stretching

About Heart Disease

Heart disease occurs due to blocked arteries and high cholesterol. A heart-healthy diet focuses on reducing saturated fats and increasing fiber-rich foods to improve heart function.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Oats and whole grains
  • Fresh fruits (apple, berries)
  • Green leafy vegetables
  • Low-fat dairy
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Legumes (dal, beans)

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Butter and ghee (excess)
  • Fried foods
  • Refined flour (maida)
  • Processed snacks
  • High-sugar foods

Diet Plan 🍽️

  • Breakfast: Oats / vegetable poha / vegetable dalia / fruit bowl
  • Mid-Morning: Apple / orange / nuts / green tea
  • Lunch: Brown rice + dal + sabzi + salad / roti + paneer + vegetables
  • Snack: Roasted chana / fruits / sprouts salad
  • Dinner: Vegetable soup / khichdi / roti + light sabzi

Exercises 🏃

  • Brisk walking
  • Yoga
  • Light cycling
  • Breathing exercises

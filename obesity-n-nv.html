NutriSmart

About Obesity

Including lean protein in North Indian diets helps control appetite and reduces overeating. Proper meal balance and reduced oil intake are key to effective weight management.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Grilled chicken (low oil)
  • Boiled or omelette eggs
  • Fish curry (less oil)
  • Whole wheat roti (limited)
  • Vegetables and salads
  • Low-fat dairy

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Butter chicken, creamy gravies
  • Fried chicken
  • Red meat (frequent consumption)
  • Excess oil and ghee
  • Fast food and processed meat

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast Options: Boiled eggs / omelette (less oil) / oats + milk / vegetable poha
  • Mid-Morning: Apple / orange / buttermilk
  • Lunch Options: 2 roti + grilled chicken / fish curry (less oil) + sabzi + salad
  • Evening Snack: Roasted chana / sprouts / green tea
  • Dinner Options: Grilled chicken / light soup + sautéed vegetables / egg whites
  • Optional: Lemon water / coconut water (no sugar)

Why This Plan Works 📊

  • Protein-rich meals reduce hunger and cravings
  • Controls calorie intake without extreme dieting
  • Balanced meals prevent overeating
  • Encourages sustainable weight loss

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Walking/jogging (40 min)
  • Strength training (3 times/week)
  • Cycling or sports
  • Daily activity goals

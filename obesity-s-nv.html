NutriSmart

About Obesity

Including lean protein in South Indian diets helps improve satiety and reduces overeating. Proper portion control and balanced meals are key to weight loss.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Grilled fish, boiled eggs
  • Skinless chicken (low oil)
  • Millets and brown rice
  • Vegetable-based curries
  • Salads and soups
  • Nuts in moderation

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Fried chicken/fish
  • Red meat (frequent consumption)
  • High oil curries
  • White rice in large portions
  • Fast food and processed meat

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast Options: Idli / dosa (less oil) + egg / omelette / upma
  • Mid-Morning: Coconut water / banana / papaya
  • Lunch Options: Brown rice / millet rice + fish curry / chicken (low oil) + vegetable poriyal
  • Evening Snack: Sundal / roasted peanuts (limited) / buttermilk
  • Dinner Options: Grilled fish / egg whites + vegetables / light soup
  • Optional: Rasam / herbal drinks

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Brisk walking/jogging (40 min)
  • Strength training (3x/week)
  • Cycling or sports activity
  • Daily movement goal

Health Tip 💡

Protein-rich meals help reduce hunger and support fat loss. Avoid deep frying.

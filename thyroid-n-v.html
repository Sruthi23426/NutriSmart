About Thyroid

Thyroid disorders affect metabolism and energy levels. A vegetarian diet rich in iodine, selenium, and zinc helps regulate thyroid function and maintain hormonal balance.

Foods to Include ✅

  • Iodized salt
  • Milk, paneer, curd
  • Nuts (especially walnuts, almonds)
  • Whole grains
  • Green vegetables (cooked)
  • Fruits like banana, apple

Foods to Avoid ❌

  • Raw cruciferous vegetables (cabbage, cauliflower)
  • Highly processed foods
  • Excess soy products
  • Refined sugar

Diet Plan Structure 🍽️

  • Breakfast Options: Oats with milk / vegetable poha / paneer paratha / fruit smoothie
  • Mid-Morning: Banana / apple / soaked nuts / fruit bowl
  • Lunch Options: 2 roti + paneer curry + vegetables / dal + rice + curd / rajma + roti
  • Evening Snack: Roasted chana / sprouts / nuts / herbal tea
  • Dinner Options: Vegetable khichdi / roti + sabzi + curd / soup + paneer

Recommended Exercises 🏃

  • Yoga
  • Walking
  • Light strength training
